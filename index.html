Component Hierarchy

Atomic Design → Vue. Каждый уровень строится из предыдущего.

Atoms Primitives Button, Input, Badge, Avatar, Icon, Spinner components/common/
Molecules Components SearchInput, Select, ConfirmDialog, EmptyState components/common/
Organisms Patterns Sidebar, ConversationList, PipelineBoard components/
Templates Layouts MainLayout, AuthLayout layouts/
Pages Views Dashboard, Conversations, Integrations, Plans views/
